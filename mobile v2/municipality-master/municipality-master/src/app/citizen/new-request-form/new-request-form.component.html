License for permanent and temporary advertising
Required Documents:
Decree No. 8861/96, Article 7 / (Regulating and Licensing Ads).
1- A request signed by the person concerned or his representative legally.
2- Real estate statement of the property in which the advertisement will be placed, not more than three months old.
3- A planning and easement statement not more than three months old.
4- Insurance policy for the board against others.
5- A document proving that the advertisement is not illuminated by infringing on energy networks.
6- A contract with the property owner or his approval registered with the notary, or a license from the competent department if the billboard is to be placed in public property or a registered rental contract in the municipality.
7- Three copies of the site map on the scale of 1/100 and the map of the billboard on the scale of 1/20 showing the measurements of the board and its height from the ground, the method of fixing it and the materials made of it.
8- Three copies of the construction facade photographer on a scale of 1/50 showing the construction lines and how to install the advertisement on it.
9 - A photograph of the site showing the road and the surrounding environment, with the actual location of the board being observed on the image.
10- A secretion map of the storey on the ground floors, in case the property is excreted for permanent advertisement.
11- A copy of the occupancy license to verify the legality of the construction, in the event that the construction was completed after 9/13/1971. (The transaction file includes a license form for the permanent or temporary announcement.)

Completion time:
Decree No. 8861/96 Article / 8 / (organizing and licensing advertisements).
1- The file is transmitted within three days from the date of its registration with the concerned licensing department to the technical department in the municipality or the Federation of Municipalities if it exists, either to a branch or civil organization office in the governorate or the judiciary to study it and express the technical opinion and return it to the valid reference for granting the license during a period 15 days from the date it was received.
2- The concerned departments must, within three days of receiving the approval of the technical department, notify the applicant of a notice of the value of the fees due or send him the notice by registered mail with a receipt notification. He must pay the fees due within a week of receiving the order to pay, subject to reimbursement of the license application. The license is given by the competent authority within a maximum period of one week from the date of joining the receipt for payment to the license file. If the license is not given a week's notice, the billboard subject of the request will be deemed licensed.
3- The person concerned must abide by the third paragraph of the first article of this decree under the penalty of removing the advertisement at his expense and responsibility, and in the event of a repeat of the violation, the license shall be canceled without the owner having the right to demand any compensation.
4- Subject to the provisions of Article X of this decree, preference in licensing is given for the first time to the summoner who had a previous license and had previously installed the first legally licensed plate in the same location.
